About Us

Welcome to 225 Degrees, where the flavors of Southeast Texas meet the heart of North Carolina! Our journey began where the sizzle of Texas-style BBQ and the zesty Cajun spices danced in the air. Packed with passion and a hint of wanderlust, we embarked on a road trip to bring the bold, mouthwatering tastes of the Gulf Coast to the East.

As the wheels turned and the landscape changed, so did our menu – a harmonious blend of Texas BBQ, Cajun delights, and classic American comfort food. Picture smoky briskets slow-cooked to perfection, finger-lickin' good ribs, and Cajun classics that'll transport you straight to the bayous.

So, whether you're a Texan at heart, a North Carolinian by residence, or simply a lover of good food and good times, join us on this flavor-packed journey. We're not just serving up meals; we're sharing a taste of our roots, one plate at a time. Welcome to the crossroads of Texas BBQ, Cajun spice, and the warm embrace of North Carolina – welcome to 225 Degrees. Let's eat, y'all!
